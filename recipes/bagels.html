Bagel Recipe

Description

This recipe has a special place in my heart, for it was crafted by my great-great-great-great-great uncle in the year 1178. He had spent much of his life eating delicious, perfect bagels fresh from a bakery. He vowed to create something much more medoicre, for the average home cook. It was this mission that prompted him to travel many miles, using various means of transport to land on his fabled destination, the land of Walmart.

The bagels you shall eat upon completion of this recipe are soaked in such a profound history you may not be able to comprehend it fully, until many years later. However, for now, enjoy your bagel(s)!


Ingredients List

  • Store Bought Bagels
  • Electricity
  • Working Oven

Steps

  1. Enter your gas-powered vehicle of choice (electric vehicles not recommended)
  2. Visit your local grocery of choice
  3. Purchase a bag of pre-made bagels
  4. Return to your humble abode
  5. Place bagels into an electric powered oven
  6. Heat the oven to 70 degrees Celsius
  7. Bake your bagels for 2 hours
  8. Eat your bagels
